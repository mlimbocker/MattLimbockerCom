ToddLimbocker.com is my first venture in to making a real website for a real business. Up until making this website, my web development experience was limited to school projects, and I wanted something out on the internet that was really and truly mine. The result was a website for my dad's real estate business that he uses to advertise the homes he has listed as well as give prospective clients a little information about himself.

I made ToddLimbocker.com with a few goals in mind:

  • Compliance with current W3C standards for HTML5 and CSS
  • Clean, professional design
  • Easy to use, intuitive navigation and controls
  • Clear presentation of information with minimal visual or textual distractions
