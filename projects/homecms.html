Home CMS is my current personal project. While making a website for my father's real estate business, I discovered that making a website that can be easily updated by someone with little to no HTML experience seems to be nearly impossible. My goal for Home CMS is to make this change.

Since I spent nearly a year working in a real estate office, I have a pretty good feel for how the business works and what REALTORs would need in a website, so I thought that would be a good focus for my project. Keeping the needs of REALTORs in mind this is what I'm setting out to do with Home CMS:

  • Keep everything technical behind a pretty curtain
  • Make the interface as intuitive as possible
  • Give the user numerous design templates to choose from, but also let them use their own
  • Make Home CMS usable to as wide a user base as possible
