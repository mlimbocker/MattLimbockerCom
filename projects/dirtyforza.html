The Dirty Forza website was my first attempt at building a complete website from the ground up. I wanted to make something that would be meaningful both to myself and my friends. This turned in to an informational website about the bi-weekly video game RaceNights that my friends and I hold.

Dirty Forza started out as a class project, but I have also used it as a sandbox of sorts to try out new design and coding tricks that I have learned over the years.
